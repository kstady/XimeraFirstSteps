\begin{question}
A normal seated adult breathes in and exhales about 0.8 L of air every 4 seconds.  An experiment produced the graph below.  The volume $V$ of air
in the lungs $t$ seconds after exhaling can be approximated by a cosine function of the form $V= A \cos Bt + D, $  $0 \le t \le 8.$   Find the equation.
